Are these notaries identified, trusted parties, or they can be anonymous nodes? For a successful attack on the blockchain, you need 51% of computing power, or stakes, etc. but in this case you need to control only the 51% of these notaries (to control the DHT entries which stores hash of your chain head). So, you have to trust in the notaries (the DHT), or if it is a trustless network, an attack can be easier, because you need to control only the 51% of notaries instead of 51% of the whole network. Is it right?
